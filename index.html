My name is Margaret Pope. I graduated from the University of Mississippi in 2016 with a degree in history. Upon graduation, I moved to Charlottesville, Virginia, where I worked for a small organization called Mockingbird for three years. In late 2019, I moved back to my hometown, Birmingham, Alabama, where I work at the church I grew up attending. I tend to be the catch-all person at work, so my position includes a variety of responsibilities, including adminstrative assistant, social media management, and membership database maintenance.

In my free time, I to visit with my friends and family. During the Covid lockdown, I picked up needlepoint and cross-stitch which continue to be favorite hobbies of mine. I started a stitch club to meet women my age who also enjoy needlework. I also like to travel, especially the planning and logistics side of traveling. My favorites places I have visited are:

  1. Israel
  2. Austin, TX
  3. Highlands, NC
  4. Carmel, CA

My goal through this master's program is to consolidate my role into the communications department to delve more into social media and website management. I eventually want to become the Director of Communications, as well as freelance on the side. On a more personal level, I hope this program will further develop the passion I have for design and communications.
